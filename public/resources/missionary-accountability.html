Friends, a short one this week but a real one.

Tuesday night over dinner, a student named Priya — a second-year master’s student from India — opened up for the first time since arriving in August. She told me she hasn’t felt seen here. We didn’t solve anything. We just sat with it.

Keep praying for her. The trust she showed me on Tuesday is fragile and precious. Two practical things: Priya needs a ride from the airport Saturday at midnight — reply if you or anyone in your church could cover it. And pray for rhythm for me; I’m feeling a little scattered and want to be present with students, not half-here.

Pray with me this week

  • For Priya — that the door she opened Tuesday stays open.
  • For an airport ride Saturday at midnight.
  • For focus and rhythm in a scattered week.

Thank you for being in this with me. You are not just funding ministry — you are doing it.

In the work with you,
