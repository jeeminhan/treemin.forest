How do visitors reach About, Resume, Creative, Contact?

The dock is gone — where do these live in the tree metaphor?

A

Part of the Tree

About, Resume, Creative, Contact are special nodes on the tree — maybe at the trunk, or as their own small branches. They open inline panels just like projects. Everything is part of one unified tree. No separate nav.

B

Minimal Top Nav

A thin, minimal header bar with your name on the left and About / Resume / Creative / Contact links on the right. Apple-style — barely there, clean typography. The tree is the hero; nav is just utility.

C

Roots as Navigation

The roots of the tree ARE the navigation. Each root strand leads to: About (who you are), Resume (your experience), Creative (your art), Contact (reach out). Scrolling down past the branches to the roots reveals these sections. Poetic — your foundations ground your work.
