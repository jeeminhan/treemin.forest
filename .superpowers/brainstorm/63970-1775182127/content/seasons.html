How should seasons work?

You mentioned sakura — should the tree change throughout the year?

A

Auto-Season (Real Calendar)

The tree automatically matches the current real-world season. Spring: sakura petals falling, pink accents. Summer: full green canopy, warm tones. Autumn: falling leaves, amber/orange. Winter: bare branches, snow particles, cool grays. Visitors see the tree as it would be "right now."

B

Always Sakura

The tree is permanently in spring. Sakura petals drift down constantly. Pink accent color throughout. It's your signature look — the Japan connection, the beauty, the falling petals as ambient motion. No seasonal switching needed.

C

Toggle / Easter Egg

Default is sakura (spring), but there's a subtle toggle or interaction that lets visitors cycle through seasons. Maybe clicking the trunk, or a small season icon. A discovery moment — rewards the curious.
