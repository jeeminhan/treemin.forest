What visual style for the tree?

Clean/geometric/futuristic Apple aesthetic — but which flavor?

A. Glass Botanical

Frosted glass nodes on precise geometric branches. Off-white background, monochrome palette. Nodes glow subtly on hover. Apple product page meets botanical diagram. Sakura petals fall as translucent glass shards.

B. Neon Circuit Tree

Dark background, neon-colored branches per cluster. Circuit-trace aesthetics with right-angle bends. Nodes pulse gently. Cyberpunk meets tree of life. Sakura petals glow as they fall. Each cluster has its own color.

C. Minimal Line Art

Thin, organic curves. Monochrome with one muted accent (soft pink for sakura). Maximum whitespace. Nodes are just outlined circles with text. Apple keynote minimalism meets Japanese ink drawing. Sakura as delicate pink ellipses drifting down.
