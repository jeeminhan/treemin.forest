How should the tree grow as you scroll?

The tree is the central navigation — how you scroll determines the whole feel

A. Bottom-up Growth

Start at the roots (education, values). Scroll up through the trunk. Branches with projects appear as you climb. The crown at top is your identity/vision. Feels like growing — your journey from foundations to where you are now.

B. Top-down Discovery

Start with your name and tagline at the crown. Scroll down through branches (projects). End at roots (foundations). Feels like exploring — visitors discover your work first, then understand what grounds it.

C. Full Tree + Scroll Panels

The entire tree is visible in the hero viewport. Clicking a branch scrolls you to a detail section below. Branches animate/highlight on hover. Feels like a map — see everything, then dive in.
